FAQ
Common questions
Everything you need to know before getting started.
What data sources does Sierra Trading use? ▼

We pull data from NASDAQ screener APIs, SEC EDGAR (8-K filings), Finviz, and Yahoo Finance. Stock quotes update in real-time during market hours, and the pre-market scanner runs automatically before the bell.

How often is the data updated? ▼

The scanner refreshes automatically throughout the trading session. Pre-market data starts populating around 4 AM ET. Catalyst data (news and filings) is fetched on-demand when you click a ticker, so it's always current.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. Cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings and you won't be charged again. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period.

Is this a trading signals or alerts service? ▼

No. Sierra Trading is a research and scanning tool, not a signals service. We surface data, catalysts, and sector context to help you make your own informed decisions. We don't provide buy/sell recommendations.

What stocks does the scanner cover? ▼

The scanner focuses on NASDAQ and NYSE-listed stocks in the $1-$20 range with an emphasis on low-float, high-volatility names. It's purpose-built for penny stock and small-cap day traders.
